12.1 KAh naut nógquod mish monchanatam8onk ut kesukqut, mittamwossis hogk8 nepauzoh, kah nanepaushadtoh agwe wusseetut, kah ut uppuhkukqut chetuhquab[eu/esu] nabo neesuoh anogqsoh.
12.2 Kah wompequait, mauoup neechadt, kah onkquommamwit wunneechanat.
12.3 Neit onkatog nógquot monchanatam8onk ut kesukqut, kah kusseh mussugken musquesu dragon, nesausuk tohshontupau, kah piogquoshkonau, kah nesausuk tahshinash chetuhquabeash ut uppuhkukquehtu.
12.4 Kah wussugqun wutonchimau nishwe chippissinitcheh anogqsoh kesukqut, kah wuttinohkonuh ohkeit, kah dragon neepau anaquabit mittamwossis, noh nahen neechop, ummohchippónat ummukkiésoh quenau nekinit.
12.5 Kah neechau mukkutchouksoh noh woh nanauwunuk wame wutohtimóneash, nashpe moóhshogque souobpunk. & ummukkachouxoh ushpunau en Godut, kah ut wutappuonganit.
12.6 Kah mittamwossis spuhh8au touohkomuk, uttoh adt wutápeuonganit quaquoshwétamauont God, onk woh nag wuttit assâmóneau nequt muttannongane, kah nese pasuk kah nequtta tahshinchge kesukodash.
12.7 Kah na mô ayeuwuttuonk ut kesukqut, Michael kah wutangelumoh, ayeuhkonáóg dragonoh, kah dragon ayeuhteau kah wut[-]angelsumoh.
12.8 Kah matta sohkoseu, kah matta wonk namehteauouun wutayeuonk ut kesukqut.
12.9 Kah masugkenuk dragon sohwhohkon, noh nukkone ask8k, uss8wesu mattannit & Satan: noh as8kekodtog wame muttaok, mô sohwhohkon en ohkeit, kah wutangelsumoh weeche sohwhohkonuh.
12.10 Kah nunn8tam mishe wadtauatonkqussuonk, n8wadt kesukqut, eyeu peyaum8 wadchanittuonk kah menuhkesuonk, kah wuttass8tam8onk num-Manitt8un, kah ummenuhkesuonk uk-Christumoh: newutche noh ashpunnánont nemattinnónuh, penuhkon, noh agapunánont anaquabit num-Manitt8mun kesukodae kah nukonáe.
12.11 Kah nag wussohkauóuh nashpe 8sqheonk lambe, kah nashpe wuttinn8waonk 8wauwaongan8, kah matta womantam8og uppomantamongan8 en nuppunat.
12.12 Newutche wekontam8k kenaau kesukquog, kah kenaau na wadohkeógish; woe en nag wadohkitcheg óhkeit, kah keihtahhannet, newutche mattannit n8keu en kuhhogkaóut m8cheke musquantog, newutche wahteau wuttutt8chéeiyeum tiohqui.
12.13 Kah dragon nag* unnohkon ohkeit, n8swetahwau mittamwussissoh, noh neechop mukkutchuxoh.
12.14 Kah mittamwossis aninnumauau neesuoh wunnuppuhwhunuh m[í]she wompsikuk, onk woh wuttinnunnónat touohkomuk, en wutayeuonganit, uttoh adt sohkom8nut, utt8cheyeu, kah utt8cheyeu8ash, & pohshe utt8cheyeu8, wutch wuskesukqut ask8k.
12.15 Kah ask8k menadtam wutch wutt8nut nippe onatuh tomogkon, wun8sukâuónat mittamwossissoh: onk woh ukquohchipwunónat wutch tomogkon.
12.16 Kah ohke aninnumauau mittamwossissoh, kah ohke woshwunum wutt8n kah misheask8 tomogkon ne dragon manadtog wutch wutt8nut.
12.17 Kah dragon musquanumau mittamwossissoh, kah mónchu ayeuhkonau, neg agguhsinitche 8skannemunneat, neg nanauwéhteunkeg wutann8hteám8onk God, kah ohtunkig 8wauwáonk Jesus Christ.

22.18 Newutche n8wauwaontamau nish$noh wosketomp, noh n8tog quosháe missohhamwe kutt8wongash yeuut b8kut, howan yeush k8t[n]e n8wadt, God pish ukk8tnumauoh aninneaongash nish wasukuhwhósikish yeuut b8kut.
22.19 Kah howan amaunuk wutch quosháe missohhamwe kutt8wóngash yeuut b8k, God pish amaunum wutchippiyeum, wutch pomantamóe b8kut, kah wutch wunnetupanatamwe otanat, kah wutch nish wasukuhwhosikish yeuut b8kut.
22.20 Noh wauwadt yeush n8wau, wunnamuhkut teanuk nuppeyam Amen, ne naj, peyaush Lord Jesus.
